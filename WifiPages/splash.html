Welcome to the University of Auckland node on the New Zealand Virtual World Grid!

This website allows you to view your account, inventory and other information specific to the University of Auckland installation. The grid has been established with an academic focus and will be used for research and education, as well as for proof-of-concept testing.

New Accounts

When you apply for a new account, we do require that you provide your real name and contact information. You are welcome to select an avatar name different from your actual name, and are welcome to have more than one account if necessary. Please contact the grid administrator below to request multiple accounts.
